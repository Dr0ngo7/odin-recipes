Menemen Recipes

The best breakfast

Menemen is a common breakfast dish in Turkey. It's easy to make and tastes top notch.

Ingredients(for 1-2 people)

  • 1 Tomato
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Pepper
  • Half spoon of oil
  • 2 Egg

Steps

  1. First, the tomatoes and onions must be peeled and cut into cubes.
  2. Second, the peppers should be finely chopped.
  3. Heat the oil in the pan, then fry the onions and peppers for 2 minutes over medium heat.
  4. Add the tomatoes and cook for 2 minutes.
  5. Add eggs, salt and any seasonings of your choice.
  6. Stir for 30 seconds on low heat.
  7. Ready to serve hot with toasted bread.
