Ingredients

-For dough-

  • 3 Water glass flour (3 x 200ml)
  • A Water glass warm water
  • 1 Egg
  • A tea spoon salt

-For internal mortar-

  • 250 gram mince
  • 1 Onion
  • 1/2 Tea spoon black pepper
  • A tea spoon salt
  • 1/2 Tea spoon hot pepper

-For the sauce-

  • 2 Tablespoons of butter
  • 2 Tablespoons of tomato paste

Steps

  1. Take the dough ingredients in a bowl and knead.
  2. Let the dough rest.
  3. For the mortar, grate 1 onion and add it to the minced meat.
  4. Then add black pepper, salt and hot pepper and mix well.
  5. Form many balls from the dough and roll them out with a rolling pin.
  6. Cut the dough into small squares.
  7. Place a small piece of mortar in the center of each square.
  8. Holding one end of the squares, fold them into a triangle.
  9. Add water to a saucepan, bring it to a boil and cook the ravioli for about 20 minutes.
  10. For the sauce, melt 2 tablespoons of butter in a bowl.
  11. Add 2 tablespoons of tomato paste and water to the melted butter.
  12. After draining the ravioli on the serving plate, pour the garlic yoghurt on it.
  13. After pouring the sauce you prepared on it, you can serve it by adding dry mint, sumac and chili peppers. Enjoy your meal.
