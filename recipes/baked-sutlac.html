Baked Sütlaç Recipes

A kind of pudding

It's supposedly sweet but a giant calorie bomb with rice in it.

Ingredients

  • 5 glasses of milk
  • 1 cup of granulated sugar
  • A Tea cup rice
  • 2 Cups water (for boiling rice)
  • 1 Egg yolk
  • 2 Tablespoon wheat starch
  • A package vanillin

Steps

  1. Put cold milk and granulated sugar in a large saucepan. After mixing with a whisk until the granulated sugar dissolves, take it to the stove. Let it simmer on low heat.
  2. Add 2 cups of water to the rice in a saucepan and bring it to a boil over medium heat.
  3. Add vanilla to milk.
  4. Remove the boiled rice from the stove and drain the excess water.
  5. Add the boiled rice into the sugary milk boiling for 1-2 minutes.
  6. In a bowl, whisk together the egg yolk, wheat starch and 1 glass of water.
  7. Slowly add the egg yolk mixture you prepared to the rice mixture and mix quickly.
  8. Pour the thickened Sütlaç evenly into heatproof portioned casserole dishes.
  9. Arrange the Sütlaç bowls spaced apart on a deep baking tray. Add cold water to half of the containers.
  10. Bake the Sütlaç in a preheated 200 degree oven for 12-15 minutes.
  11. Remove the Sütlaç bowls from the oven with the help of a kitchen glove, cool at room temperature and put them in the refrigerator. Share it with your loved ones after it cools down.
